# PawnBroker Store Policies

## Jewelry Appraisal Rules
* **Gold:** Always check for hallmarks (10k, 14k, 18k). 
* **Loan Value:** Offer **60%** of the current spot price for scrap gold.
* **Testing:** All diamonds over 0.5ct must be tested with a thermal probe.

## Electronics Rules
* **Laptops:** Must include original charger. 
* **Testing:** Verify the device is not "Cloud Locked" or "Find My" enabled.
* **Loan Value:** Offer **40%** of the average "Sold" price on eBay.
